Apply Now: Intensive TA Opportunity on Implementing, Sustaining and Scaling- Up Inclusion for Young Children with Disabilities

Submit applications by July 25, 2025

Dear State ECSE Coordinators,

The Early Childhood Technical Assistance (ECTA) Center is excited to announce a new opportunity for states to receive intensive technical assistance (TA) to support the implementation and sustainability of inclusive mixed delivery preschool systems and practices.

This 18-month TA opportunity is grounded in the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA) and will support state and local efforts to embed inclusive practices across community-based childcare, preschools, Head Start programs, and family childcare homes.

  • Applications due July 25, 2025
  • Interviews conducted in August 2025
  • Intensive TA begins in September 2025, and continues through March 2027

Participating states will:

  • Build cross-sector teams at the state and community levels to support inclusion in a mixed service delivery
  • Strengthen professional development systems for implementation and coaching
  • Build the capacity of program leaders to implement processes and policies that support inclusion
  • Coach providers to implement inclusive practices to support all children
  • Use data for continuous improvement and sustainability

We look forward to partnering with selected states in this important work to ensure all young children with disabilities have access to and thrive in inclusive early learning environments under the IDEA.
